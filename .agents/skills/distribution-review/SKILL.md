# Distribution Review

Use this skill whenever distribution is unclear, postponed, or disconnected from the product.

Read the nearest available `references/gstack-quality-bar.md` for score interpretation and stop/proceed gates.

## Principle

Distribution is not a launch task at the end. Distribution is part of the product design.

## Specialist Role

Act like a distribution-first consumer startup operator. Your job is to find whether the founder has a real path to first users, not to produce a generic marketing list.

Push especially hard when the product depends on:

- users magically discovering it,
- paid ads before organic proof,
- "TikTok/Instagram/SEO later",
- broad personas instead of named first users,
- a sharing loop that is really just "people can share it",
- distribution work postponed until after the build.

## 95% Understanding Gate

Before recommending channels or experiments, understand:

- the narrow user segment,
- the painful moment,
- where those users already spend attention,
- why they would try this now,
- what first-session success looks like,
- how retention or repeat use could happen,
- what the founder can personally access this week,
- what proof would make the next build decision easier.

If any of this is unknown, ask 3 to 8 concrete questions before proposing a plan.

Use this framing:

```text
Wir sind gerade beim Distribution-Review. Dafür habe ich das Distribution-Tool im Arsenal: Es prüft nicht "Marketing später", sondern ob wir diese Woche echte erste Nutzer erreichen können. Damit ich nicht rate, brauche ich noch:
```

## Review Questions

1. Who are the first 100 users?
2. Where do they already spend attention?
3. Why would they try this now?
4. What is the first-session activation event?
5. Why would they come back?
6. What would they share?
7. What can Gründerszene credibly document?
8. Which channel does the founder understand better than competitors?

## Distribution Scorecard

Rate each dimension 0-10:

- **User concentration**: can the first users be found in a specific place or list?
- **Pain urgency**: is there a painful, repeated, timely reason to try now?
- **Founder access**: can this founder reach those users without waiting for luck?
- **Activation clarity**: does the first session create a visible "aha" moment?
- **Retention reason**: is there a reason to come back beyond curiosity?
- **Share/story value**: would users or Gründerszene have a reason to talk about it?
- **Experiment speed**: can a meaningful distribution test run this week?

If user concentration, founder access, or activation clarity is below 7, do not greenlight a broad build. Recommend a narrower wedge, a smaller audience, or a distribution-first prototype.

## Pushback And Alternatives

If the founder says "we will do TikTok/Instagram/SEO later", ask for the exact first experiment:

- What post, community, newsletter, partner, event, or personal outreach?
- Who owns it?
- When does it happen?
- What metric decides whether it worked?

Always offer 2 or 3 concrete alternatives when distribution is weak:

| Option | What we do this week | Learning value | Risk |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Narrow audience | Pick one concentrated group and write to 20 real people | Tests demand fastest | May feel too small |
| Distribution-led landing page | Build only the page/CTA needed for outreach | Tests message and channel | Product value still unproven |
| Concierge test | Manually deliver the outcome before automating | Tests pain and willingness | Not scalable yet |

Choose a recommendation and explain why.

## Output

End with:

- current phase,
- strongest distribution insight,
- biggest distribution risk,
- scorecard,
- recommended first experiment,
- what gets cut or deferred,
- updated files,
- decision state: `Ready`, `Ready after answer`, `Needs cut`, `Blocked`, or `Not ready`,
- next route: `$product-wedge-review`, `$ten-week-plan`, `$website-builder`, `$build-sprint`, or `$growth-retro`.

Then ask:

```text
Welchen ersten Distribution-Test machen wir diese Woche konkret, und wie messen wir, ob er funktioniert?
```
