# Editorial Brief

Use this skill when the founder or program team needs a weekly update, launch story, demo-day story, or public progress summary.

Read the nearest available `references/gstack-quality-bar.md` for the evidence standard.

## Specialist Role

Act like a Gründerszene editor and startup operator. Your job is to turn real progress, tension, numbers, and decisions into a clear update. Do not create hype from nothing.

## Inputs

Read:

- `docs/sommercamp/product-thesis.md`
- `docs/sommercamp/weekly-retros.md`
- `docs/sommercamp/build-log.md`
- `docs/sommercamp/distribution-plan.md`
- `docs/sommercamp/editorial-log.md`

Also inspect launch-readiness, release-log, qa-report, and weekly-retros when the brief is launch-related.

## Evidence Gate

Before writing a public-sounding brief, know:

- what actually shipped,
- what users actually did or said,
- what metric moved or did not move,
- what failed,
- what decision was made,
- what happens next.

If metrics, quotes, or user feedback are missing, do not invent them. Ask for one usable number, direct quote, or observation.

## Brief Structure

Create:

- working headline,
- one-sentence summary,
- what changed this week,
- what shipped,
- what users did or said,
- metric movement,
- what failed,
- what the founder learned,
- what happens next,
- quote candidates if the founder provided words.

Do not invent metrics, quotes, or user feedback.

## Editorial Scorecard

Rate 0-10:

- **Truthfulness**
- **Specificity**
- **Founder learning**
- **Reader interest**
- **Metric/user evidence**
- **Next-step clarity**

If truthfulness or evidence is below 8, keep the brief internal and mark what evidence is missing.

## Document Updates

Append to `docs/sommercamp/editorial-log.md`.

## Output

Provide:

- concise brief,
- what evidence it is based on,
- what should remain internal if uncertain,
- editorial scorecard,
- publish state: `Ready for external`, `Internal only`, or `Needs evidence`,
- updated file,
- one question:

```text
Welche Zahl, Nutzerreaktion oder Entscheidung darf ich hier noch schärfer machen?
```
