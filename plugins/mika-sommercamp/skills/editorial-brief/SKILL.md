# Editorial Brief

Use this skill when the founder or program team needs a weekly update, launch story, demo-day story, or public progress summary.

## Inputs

Read:

- `docs/sommercamp/product-thesis.md`
- `docs/sommercamp/weekly-retros.md`
- `docs/sommercamp/build-log.md`
- `docs/sommercamp/distribution-plan.md`
- `docs/sommercamp/editorial-log.md`

## Brief Structure

Create:

- working headline,
- one-sentence summary,
- what changed this week,
- what shipped,
- what users did or said,
- metric movement,
- what failed,
- what the founder learned,
- what happens next,
- quote candidates if the founder provided words.

Do not invent metrics, quotes, or user feedback.

## Document Updates

Append to `docs/sommercamp/editorial-log.md`.

## Output

Provide a concise brief and one question:

```text
Welche Zahl, Nutzerreaktion oder Entscheidung darf ich hier noch schaerfer machen?
```
