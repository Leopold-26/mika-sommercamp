# Distribution Review

Use this skill whenever distribution is unclear, postponed, or disconnected from the product.

## Principle

Distribution is not a launch task at the end. Distribution is part of the product design.

## Review Questions

1. Who are the first 100 users?
2. Where do they already spend attention?
3. Why would they try this now?
4. What is the first-session activation event?
5. Why would they come back?
6. What would they share?
7. What can Gründerszene credibly document?
8. Which channel does the founder understand better than competitors?

## Pushback

If the founder says "we will do TikTok/Instagram/SEO later", ask for the exact first experiment:

- What post, community, newsletter, partner, event, or personal outreach?
- Who owns it?
- When does it happen?
- What metric decides whether it worked?
